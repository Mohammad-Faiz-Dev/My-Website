About Me

I'm Mohammad Faiz, a self-taught and passionate developer from Lucknow, India. I love turning simple ideas into interactive, animated, and visually compelling web experiences using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Python.

Why Web Development?

I enjoy creating intuitive and responsive user interfaces. The satisfaction of bringing something to life on the screen with just lines of code keeps me going. I experiment with toggle themes, scroll effects, animations, and API integration to make my websites fun and functional.

My Values

I'm a fast learner who believes in clean code, minimalistic design, and attention to detail. I believe the web should be accessible, fast, and visually pleasing — and I aim to bring all of that into every project.

Hobbies & Interests

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me exploring tech tools like Blackbox.ai, learning new shortcuts, improving my typing speed, or diving into fun Python mini-games. I also enjoy movies, coffee, and late-night debugging marathons.
